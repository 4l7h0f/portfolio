# metrics.md

## 1. Visibility Metrics

Measure how often your brand appears in search & AI systems:

* Organic impressions
* Keyword rankings
* AI-generated mentions (manual tracking)
* Featured snippets / rich results presence

---

## 2. Traffic Metrics

Measure actual visits:

* Organic traffic
* Direct traffic (brand strength indicator)
* Referral traffic (from communities/platforms)

---

## 3. Engagement Metrics

Measure content quality:

* Time on page
* Bounce rate
* Pages per session
* Scroll depth

---

## 4. Conversion Metrics

Measure business impact:

* Leads generated
* Sales / revenue
* Conversion rate per page
* Assisted conversions

---

## 5. Authority Metrics

Measure trust & credibility:

* Backlinks (quantity + quality)
* Referring domains
* Brand mentions (linked & unlinked)
* Domain authority (relative metric)

---

## 6. Topical Authority Metrics

Measure depth in a niche:

* Number of articles per topic cluster
* Internal link density
* Ranking coverage across related keywords

---

## 7. Brand Metrics

Measure recognition:

* Branded search volume
* Direct traffic growth
* Social mentions
* Returning visitors

---

## 8. Distribution Metrics

Measure reach outside your site:

* Engagement on LinkedIn / Reddit / etc.
* Shares and reposts
* Click-through rate from external platforms

---

## 9. Technical SEO Metrics

Measure site health:

* Index coverage
* Crawl errors
* Page speed (Core Web Vitals)
* Mobile usability

---

## 10. Experimentation Metrics

Measure learning velocity:

* Number of tests run
* Win rate of experiments
* Performance lift per test
* Time to validation

---

## Key Principle

Do not rely on a single metric.

Success = combination of:

* Visibility
* Authority
* Traffic
* Conversions

Balanced measurement ensures long-term growth.
