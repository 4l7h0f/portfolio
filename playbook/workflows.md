# workflows.md

## 1. Topic → Authority Workflow

**Step 1: Define Core Topic**

* Choose a niche with business value

**Step 2: Build Topical Map**

* Break into subtopics
* Identify relationships between topics

**Step 3: Cluster Content**

* Create pillar + supporting articles
* Ensure internal linking structure

**Output:** Topical authority structure

---

## 2. Content Production Workflow

**Step 1: Create Content Brief**

* Target entity/topic
* Search intent
* Key questions to answer

**Step 2: Write for AI + Humans**

* Clear structure (H1–H3)
* Direct answers
* Concise explanations

**Step 3: Optimize Format**

* Lists
* Tables
* Definitions
* FAQs

**Output:** AI-readable content

---

## 3. Entity Building Workflow

**Step 1: Create Entity Home**

* About page
* Clear positioning
* Structured data

**Step 2: Reinforce Across Content**

* Consistent brand mention
* Same descriptors

**Step 3: External Validation**

* Mentions on other platforms
* Profiles (LinkedIn, etc.)

**Output:** Recognizable entity

---

## 4. Distribution Workflow

**Step 1: Repurpose Content**

* Blog → LinkedIn post
* Blog → Reddit discussion
* Blog → Short video

**Step 2: Seed Content**

* Share in communities
* Start discussions

**Step 3: Amplify**

* Engage with comments
* Encourage sharing

**Output:** Multi-platform visibility

---

## 5. Internal Linking Workflow

**Step 1: Map Relationships**

* Identify related articles

**Step 2: Add Contextual Links**

* Use descriptive anchor text

**Step 3: Update Regularly**

* Add links to new content

**Output:** Strong semantic structure

---

## 6. Testing Workflow

**Step 1: Form Hypothesis**

* Example: “Short answers rank better in AI results”

**Step 2: Run Experiment**

* Control vs variation pages

**Step 3: Measure Impact**

* Traffic
* Indexing
* AI visibility

**Step 4: Iterate**

* Keep what works
* Drop what doesn’t

**Output:** Proven strategies

---

## 7. Maintenance Workflow

**Step 1: Refresh Content**

* Update outdated info

**Step 2: Improve Structure**

* Add FAQs, summaries

**Step 3: Re-distribute**

* Re-share updated content

**Output:** Sustained performance
