# principles.md

## 1. AI-First Visibility (Not Just Google Rankings)

Optimize for **AI search engines (LLMs, assistants, generative search)**, not just traditional SERPs.
Content must be understandable, extractable, and trustworthy for machines.

---

## 2. Entity-Based SEO Over Keyword SEO

Shift from keywords → **entities, topics, and relationships**.
Build clear identity around:

* Who you are
* What you do
* Why you are trusted

---

## 3. Topical Authority > Content Volume

Publishing more content ≠ better results.
Winning means:

* Deep coverage of a topic
* Strong internal linking
* Clear topical structure

---

## 4. Distribution is as Important as Creation

Content without distribution will not surface in AI systems.
Visibility requires:

* Multi-platform presence (Reddit, LinkedIn, YouTube, etc.)
* Brand mentions across the web

---

## 5. Brand Signals Drive Trust

AI systems favor **recognizable, referenced brands**.
Key signals:

* Branded searches
* Mentions across platforms
* Consistent positioning

---

## 6. Human Value + Machine Readability

Content must balance:

* Human usefulness (clarity, depth, originality)
* Machine readability (structure, formatting, semantics)

---

## 7. SEO as a System, Not Tactics

SEO is no longer a checklist.
It requires:

* Repeatable workflows
* Testing frameworks
* Continuous iteration

---

## 8. Diversification Reduces Risk

Relying only on SEO is dangerous.
Build multiple acquisition channels:

* Organic search
* Social
* Direct traffic
* Communities

---

## 9. Bottom-Funnel First Strategy

Prioritize content that drives:

* Revenue
* Conversions
* Real business outcomes

Not just traffic.

---

## 10. Continuous Testing & Adaptation

There is no static “best practice”.
Winning teams:

* Test constantly
* Adapt quickly
* Ignore outdated advice
