## Key Research Findings

### 1. Brand Recall over Page Rank
*   **Entity SEO:** AI models prioritize "recalling" brands that are frequently mentioned and trusted across the web, rather than just ranking specific pages #1 in traditional search.
*   **Brand Mentions:** Brand mentions are becoming a stronger signal than traditional backlinks for AI visibility.

### 2. Video-First Strategy
*   **YouTube is King:** YouTube is the most frequent source for LLM answers.
*   **Mention & Repurpose:** Brands should naturally mention their name in videos and then use tools (like Arvow) to repurpose those videos into "entity-rich" articles.

### 3. The "TL;DR" Rule for AI Bots
*   **Concise Data:** Every page should include a "TL;DR" or "Key Takeaways" section at the top to increase the chances of being cited in an AI Overview (AIO).
*   **Directness:** Answer the main user query within the first 100 words of the content.

### 4. Reputation Control
*   **Reputation Hubs:** AI pulls heavily from Google Business Profile, Yelp, LinkedIn, and TrustPilot.
*   **Review Control:** The number of reviews and average ratings have a significant impact on AI SEO rankings.

## Actionable 2026 AI-SEO Checklist
1.  **Direct Answer:** Answer the query in the first 100 words.
2.  **Conversational Tone:** Maintain a natural, expert-driven voice.
3.  **Automated News:** Host a "News" section summarizing industry updates to build topical authority.
4.  **Schema Markup:** Use advanced schema to define people, products, and locations (entities).
5.  **Brand Narrative Check:** Regularly prompt LLMs to see what they "know" about your brand and correct inaccuracies.

## Key Outcomes
*   **2,000% Increase in AI Traffic:** Moving from zero citations to 90+ AI Overviews via brand mention strategies.
*   **Revenue Growth:** Scaling from $166k to $491k monthly by automating content systems and focusing on topical authority.
