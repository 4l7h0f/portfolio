# Jason Barnard: The Entity Home and Brand Authority (2026)

*Source: Search Engine Land & Kalicube (March 31, 2026)*
*Link: [searchengineland.com](https://searchengineland.com)*

## Key Research Findings

### 1. The "Entity Home" Concept
*   **Central Truth:** Every brand needs a designated "Entity Home" (typically the About page or a specific brand hub) that provides the "Source of Truth" for search engines and AI models.
*   **Clarification:** The Entity Home is where you tell the AI exactly who you are, what you do, and which entities you are related to.

### 2. Shaping AI Perception
*   **Nudging the LLM:** By optimizing the Entity Home with structured data and consistent brand narratives, you "nudge" AI engines to trust and recommend your brand.
*   **Consistency:** Inconsistency across different platforms (LinkedIn vs. Website vs. Wikipedia) causes "algorithmic confusion," leading to lower visibility in AI answers.

### 3. AI Assistants as Business Tools
*   **Task Implementation:** Jason advocates for a 10-step framework for leaders to integrate AI assistants into their workflows, focusing on "Knowledge Management" as the foundation.

## Summary of 2026 Playbook
Jason Barnard argues that in the age of AI, **Identity is the new Ranking.** If an AI doesn't understand your entity, it won't cite you. Brands must focus on building a robust Knowledge Graph foundation, starting with a clear, structured Entity Home.
