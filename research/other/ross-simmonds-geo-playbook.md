# Ross Simmonds: Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) Playbook (2026)

*Source: Foundation Inc. & LinkedIn (2026)*
*Link: [foundationinc.co](https://foundationinc.co)*

## Key Research Findings

### 1. From Ranking to Recommendation
*   **RoGEO (Return on GEO):** Success is measured by how often a brand is cited and recommended by AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, etc.).
*   **Buyer Journey:** In 2026, many buyer journeys start with a conversation with an AI, not a traditional search query.

### 2. The 4 Pillars of GEO Success
*   **Authoritative Answer Assets:** Content designed to be scanned and cited (Q&A formats, H2s as prompts).
*   **Novel Data & Research:** LLMs prioritize original research, statistics, and benchmark surveys because they provide "unique verifiable info."
*   **The Reddit Factor:** AI models are heavily trained on community platforms. Brands must influence sentiment on Reddit/Quora to shape how AI "thinks" of them.
*   **EEAT Infrastructure:** Robust author pages and distribution across high-authority platforms are essential for AI trust.

### 3. The G.E.O.D.A.T.A. Framework
*   **G**ather Intelligence: Analyze current AI brand mentions.
*   **E**valuate Competitors: See who AI cites instead of you.
*   **O**ptimize Content: Add quotations, stats, and citations (proven to boost AI visibility by 40%).
*   **D**evelop Sentiment: Influence public discourse in communities.
*   **A**nalyze Performance: Track citations within LLM responses.
*   **T**arget Data Sources: Be where the AI crawlers go.
*   **A**nswer Accurately: Provide direct answers to buyer questions.

## Summary of 2026 Playbook
Ross Simmonds highlights that **Citations** are the new backlinks. To win in 2026, brands must move from "long-form blog posts" to "Answer Assets" and "Original Research." Being mentioned positively in high-trust communities like Reddit is now a top-tier SEO (or GEO) priority.
