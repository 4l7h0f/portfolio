# Where Experts Disagree

## 1. Can AI Replace SEO Consultants?

### Author A — Bernard Huang
- AI can automate large parts of SEO workflows:
  - Content optimization
  - SERP analysis
  - Topic research

- AI assists in identifying gaps and improving content performance
(source: Bernard Huang, https://www.clearscope.io/blog/ai-in-seo-what-you-need-to-know from 15.01.2026)

### Author B — Aleyda Solís

- AI cannot replace:
  - Strategy
  - Prioritization
  - Business alignment
(source: Aleyda Solís, https://learningseo.io/ from 05.09.2025)

- Consultants provide:
  - Context
  - Prioritization
  - Business understanding

### My Position:
AI replaces execution, not strategy.

### Reason:
- Detection and analysis are automatable
- Strategic decisions require human judgment and business context

## 2. What Is the Primary SEO KPI?

### Author A — Brian Dean
- Rankings and organic traffic are primary metrics
(source: Brian Dean, https://backlinko.com/google-ranking-factors from 15.11.2024)

### Author B — Kevin Indig
- SEO is shifting toward:

  - Visibility
  - Impressions
  - Influence (not just clicks)
(source: Kevin Indig, https://www.growth-memo.com from 12.03.2026)

### My Position:
Use a hybrid KPI model:
- Short-term: rankings and traffic
- Long-term: AI visibility and influence

### Reason:
- AI answers reduce clicks but increase influence
- SEO must adapt to both systems

---

## 3. Does Content Alone Still Win?

### Author A — Traditional SEO Thinking
- High-quality content + backlinks are sufficient

### Author B — Jason Barnard
- AI prioritizes:

  - Brand authority
  - Entity recognition
(source: Jason Barnard, https://kalicube.com/ from 20.01.2026)

### My Position:
Content + brand authority must be integrated

### Reason:
- AI systems prioritize trusted entities
- Unknown brands are less likely to be surfaced

## 4. How Often Should SEO Be Executed?

### Author A — Traditional Approach
- Monthly or quarterly audits

### Author B — Gael Breton

- Continuous, automated weekly optimization
(source: Gael Breton, https://www.linkedin.com/posts/gael-breton_i-dont-think-people-realize-you-can-replace-activity-7425464338192371713-ZwE1 from 05.11.2025)

### My Position:
Weekly continuous optimization

### Reason:
- Automation reduces execution cost
- Faster iteration leads to compounding gains

## 5. Is SEO Becoming Less Important Because of AI?

### Author A — Common Narrative
- AI will reduce the importance of SEO

### Author B — Bernard Huang
- AI systems rely on:

  - Indexed web content
  - Structured, high-quality information
(source: Bernard Huang, https://www.clearscope.io/blog/optimizing-for-ai-search-in-2026 from 20.02.2026)

### My Position:
SEO is evolving, not declining

### Reason:
- AI systems depend on indexed, structured, and retrievable content
- SEO becomes the foundation of AI visibility

## 6. Should You Prioritize Content Quantity or Quality?

### Author A — Matt Diggity
- Scale content production using AI and systems
- More content = more surface area for rankings
(source: Matt Diggity, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MA496wV8qjc from 22.01.2026)

### Author B — Lily Ray
- Depth and authority matter more than volume
- AI content saturation makes differentiation critical
- Expertise and original insights win long-term
(source: Lily Ray, https://www.linkedin.com/posts/lily-ray-44755615_so-much-of-what-i-see-as-the-right-way-to-activity-7397358107875524608-4MhO from 18.11.2025)

### My Position:
Use AI for scale, humans for depth

### Reason:
- AI enables efficient content production
- Human expertise creates differentiation
- Winning strategy combines both

## 7. Are Backlinks Still Important for AI SEO?

### Author A — Matt Diggity
- Backlinks remain a core ranking factor
- Combined with on-page SEO, they drive significant results
(source: Matt Diggity, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MA496wV8qjc from 22.01.2026)

### Author B — Jason Barnard
- Brand mentions and entity recognition are replacing traditional backlinks
- AI systems prioritize "ambient trust signals" across the web
(source: Jason Barnard, https://kalicube.com/ from 20.01.2026)

### My Position:
Both matter, but brand signals are growing in importance

### Reason:
- Backlinks still influence traditional rankings
- AI systems increasingly value distributed trust signals
- Diversified authority building is safest approach
