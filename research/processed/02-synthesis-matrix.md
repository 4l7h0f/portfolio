# SOP Rules — AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## 1. Keyword & Intent Rules
- Prioritize search intent over keyword volume  
(source: Brian Dean, https://backlinko.com/search-intent from 12.03.2023)

- Group keywords into topical clusters instead of targeting single keywords  
(source: Koray Tuğberk GÜBÜR, https://topicalauthority.digital from 15.06.2025)

---

## 2. SERP & AI Analysis Rules
- Analyze top-ranking pages before creating content  
- Identify structure, angle, and intent alignment  

- Evaluate how AI systems answer the query (ChatGPT, Google AI)  
(source: Google Search Central, https://developers.google.com/search/docs/fundamentals/creating-helpful-content from 18.08.2022)

---

## 3. AI Content Usage Rules
- Use AI for drafting and outlining, not final publishing  
(source: Google Search Central, https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2023/02/google-search-and-ai-content from 08.02.2023)

- Always apply human editing for:
  - Accuracy
  - Depth
  - Experience

---

## 4. Content Quality Rules
- Write content that is:
  - Clear and structured
  - Answer-focused
  - Context-rich (entities, relationships)

- Avoid:
  - Keyword stuffing
  - Generic AI-generated text  

(source: Google Search Central, https://developers.google.com/search/docs/fundamentals/creating-helpful-content from 18.08.2022)

---

## 5. AI Retrieval Optimization Rules
- Structure content for easy summarization (clear headings, direct answers)  
- Ensure factual accuracy and up-to-date information  
- Use semantic clarity and structured data  

(source: Jason Barnard, https://kalicube.com/learning-spaces/ from 10.02.2026)

---

## 6. On-Page SEO Rules
- Optimize:
  - Title tags
  - Meta descriptions
  - Headings
  - Internal links  

(source: Aleyda Solís, https://learningseo.io/ from 05.09.2025)

---

## 7. Brand Authority Rules
- Build brand presence across multiple platforms  
- Increase brand mentions (not just backlinks)  
- Maintain consistent positioning and messaging  

(source: Jason Barnard, https://kalicube.com/ from 20.01.2026)

---

## 8. Automation & Execution Rules
- Automate SEO monitoring using tools connected to Google Search Console  
- Run checks weekly instead of quarterly  

(source: Gael Breton, https://www.linkedin.com/posts/gael-breton_i-dont-think-people-realize-you-can-replace-activity-7425464338192371713-ZwE1 from 05.11.2025)

---

## 9. Quick Win Rules
Prioritize pages with:
- High impressions
- Low CTR
- Rankings between positions 3–10  

Primary actions:
- Update title tags
- Improve meta descriptions  

(source: Google Search Central, https://support.google.com/webmasters/answer/7576553 from 12.04.2023)

---

## 10. Human vs AI Responsibility Rules
- Use AI for:
  - Detection
  - Pattern recognition

- Use humans for:
  - Strategy
  - Prioritization
  - Intent interpretation  

(source: Google Search Central, https://developers.google.com/search/blog/2023/02/google-search-and-ai-content from 08.02.2023)

---

## 11. KPI & Measurement Rules
Track beyond rankings:
- AI visibility (mentions in AI answers)
- Brand presence
- Conversions  

(source: Kevin Indig, https://www.growth-memo.com from 12.03.2026)

---

## 12. Continuous Optimization Rules
- Treat SEO as ongoing operations, not one-time execution  
- Run optimization cycles weekly  
- Continuously update content  

(source: Matt Diggity, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MA496wV8qjc from 22.01.2026)

---

## 13. Topical Authority Rules
- Create a topical map covering all entities, attributes, and related queries  
- Structure content into interconnected clusters (Semantic Content Network)  
- Use clear, structured language (subject → predicate → object) for better AI extraction  

(source: Koray Tuğberk GÜBÜR, https://topicalauthority.digital from 15.06.2025)

---

## 14. AI Crawlability Rules
- Ensure critical content is available in raw HTML (not JS-dependent)  
- Verify AI crawlers are not blocked by CDN or hosting configurations  

(source: Aleyda Solís, https://humansofmartech.com/2026/01/13/202-aleyda-solis-ai-search-crawlability/ from 13.01.2026)

---

## 15. Entity Building Rules
- Create a dedicated brand hub that clearly explains who you are, what you do, and who you serve  
- Use structured data (schema) to define your entity and relationships  
- Maintain consistency across all platforms (website, social profiles, third-party sites)  

(source: Jason Barnard, https://searchengineland.com from 20.02.2026)

---

## 16. Distribution Rules
- Integrate Reddit and community platforms into SEO and content strategy  
- Create content that fits platform culture (authentic, helpful, non-promotional)  
- Monitor and participate in relevant discussions where your audience is active  

(source: Ross Simmonds, https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rosssimmonds_seoweek-contrntmarketing-strategy-activity-7324106130324934656-331s from 15.09.2025)

---

## 17. E-E-A-T Alignment Rules
- Follow SEO best practices (E-E-A-T, clarity, structure)  
- Create dedicated pages that answer specific branded queries (reviews, appearances, use cases)  
- Treat your website as the single source of truth for AI systems  

(source: Lily Ray, https://www.linkedin.com/posts/lily-ray-44755615_so-much-of-what-i-see-as-the-right-way-to-activity-7397358107875524608-4MhO from 18.11.2025)
