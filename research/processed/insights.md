### Insight from Aleyda Solís (citing Dan Petrovic)

**Insight:**
AI models like GPT-5 rely on external retrieval systems instead of storing all knowledge internally.

**SEO Implication:**
Content must be discoverable and structured for both search engines and AI systems.

**SOP Rule:**
Create content that is:
- Well-structured (clear headings)
- Factually grounded
- Updated regularly
- Easily retrievable via search systems

### Insight from Bernard Huang

**Insight:**
Search is shifting from traditional “10 blue links” to AI-generated answers (“AI Mode”), changing how users consume information.

**SEO Implication:**
Ranking alone is no longer the primary goal—content must be:
- Selected
- Summarized
- Cited  
by AI systems.

This introduces a new KPI:
→ **AI visibility / retrievability**, not just organic clicks.

**SOP Rule:**
Adapt SEO strategy to AI-first search environments by:
- Structuring content for summarization (clear sections, direct answers)
- Using semantic clarity instead of keyword stuffing
- Implementing structured data (schema, entity linking)
- Tracking AI visibility (mentions in AI answers, not just rankings)
