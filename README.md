# Research Project: AI-Powered SEO Content Production (2026)

This repository contains a comprehensive research project on the state of **AI-powered SEO content production** in 2026. The goal is to collect and synthesize the latest strategies from the world's leading practitioners to build a "2026 AI-SEO Playbook."

## Chosen Topic: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

As search engines shift from "information retrieval" to "generative AI experiences" (like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews), traditional SEO tactics are being replaced by **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** and **Entity-based Authority**. This project investigates how top experts are navigating this shift.

## Repository Structure

- `/research/sources/sources.md`: A curated list of 10 experts with links to their latest content and brief annotations.
- `/research/sources/linkedin-posts/`: Detailed summaries and insights from recent LinkedIn posts by top SEO growth advisors.
- `/research/sourcesyoutube-transcripts/`: Summaries and key findings from the most impactful YouTube videos on AI-SEO.
- `/research/sources/other/`: Deep dives into specific frameworks like GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and Semantic Content Networks.
